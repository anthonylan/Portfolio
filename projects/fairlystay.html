Fairly Stay

Two apartments transformed into holiday homes by a young family based in Alghero, Italy.

role full-stack developer

client freelance project

date November 2018

The Project

Two family run apartments, located in a two-story building, located in a quiet neighborhood where the Catalan Algherese is still spoken. Strategic area of the city from where you can reach the beach and the historic center with a short walk in just 5 minutes. An area well served by public transport that connect the city with the airport and the famous beaches of Alghero.
